# /fund

Fund operations, NAV calculations, and investor management for fund managers.

## Available Capabilities

| Area | What it does | Example prompt |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| NAV Calculation | Net asset value computation, waterfall distributions | "Calculate NAV for a fund with these positions and fee structure" |
| Fund Setup | Fund structuring, legal entity design, fee arrangements | "Design a fund structure for a European real estate vehicle" |
| Investor Onboarding | KYC/AML frameworks, subscription documents, side letters | "Create an investor onboarding checklist for institutional LPs" |
| Regulatory Compliance | AIFMD, SEC, FCA reporting requirements and templates | "What are the quarterly reporting obligations under AIFMD?" |
| Performance Reporting | IRR, TVPI, DPI calculations, investor letters | "Draft a quarterly investor letter with these performance figures" |
| Capital Calls | Drawdown notices, distribution calculations, equalization | "Calculate a capital call with equalization for a late closer" |

## Quick Start

1. "Set up a fund waterfall with an 8% preferred return and 20% carry."
2. "Generate a quarterly investor report template for a PE fund."
3. "Walk me through the regulatory requirements for launching a fund in Luxembourg."
