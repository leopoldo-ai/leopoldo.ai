# /marketing-hub

Campaigns, content strategy, segmentation, and brand management for marketing teams.

## Available Capabilities

| Area | What it does | Example prompt |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Campaign Planning | Multi-channel campaign design, budgeting, timeline management | "Plan a product launch campaign across digital and events" |
| Content Strategy | Content calendars, topic clustering, SEO-driven planning | "Build a 90-day content strategy for a B2B SaaS company" |
| Customer Segmentation | Persona development, cohort analysis, targeting frameworks | "Define customer segments for a premium fitness brand" |
| Brand Strategy | Brand architecture, messaging frameworks, tone of voice | "Develop a messaging framework for our rebrand" |
| Performance Marketing | Channel mix modeling, attribution, ROAS optimization | "Analyze our channel mix and recommend budget reallocation" |
| Go-to-Market | Launch playbooks, pricing strategy, distribution planning | "Create a GTM plan for entering the DACH market" |

## Quick Start

1. "Design a multi-channel campaign for a new enterprise product launch."
2. "Build customer personas for a D2C subscription business."
3. "Create a content calendar focused on thought leadership in AI."
