# /legal

Corporate law, contract drafting, dispute resolution, and regulatory compliance for legal professionals.

## Available Capabilities

| Area | What it does | Example prompt |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Corporate Law | Entity formation, governance, shareholder agreements, board matters | "Draft a shareholders' agreement for a joint venture" |
| Contract Drafting | Commercial agreements, NDAs, licensing, SaaS terms | "Review this SaaS agreement and flag key risk areas" |
| Dispute Resolution | Litigation strategy, arbitration, mediation frameworks | "Outline a dispute resolution strategy for this breach of contract" |
| Intellectual Property | Patent strategy, trademark filing, IP portfolio management | "Develop an IP protection strategy for a tech startup" |
| Labour and Employment | Employment contracts, policies, termination procedures | "Draft an employment contract compliant with EU regulations" |
| Real Estate | Lease review, acquisition structuring, due diligence | "Review this commercial lease and identify unfavorable terms" |
| Regulatory Compliance | GDPR, financial regulation, industry-specific compliance | "Create a GDPR compliance checklist for a data-driven product" |
| Legal Operations | Matter management, outside counsel guidelines, legal tech | "Design a legal department operating model for a scaling company" |

## Quick Start

1. "Draft a non-disclosure agreement for a technology partnership."
2. "Review this contract and summarize the key obligations and risks."
3. "Create a regulatory compliance framework for a fintech product."
