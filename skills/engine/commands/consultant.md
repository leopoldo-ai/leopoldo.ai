# /consultant

Market sizing, strategic proposals, workshops, and transformation roadmaps for consultants.

## Available Capabilities

| Area | What it does | Example prompt |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Market Sizing | TAM/SAM/SOM, top-down and bottom-up approaches | "Size the European EV charging market using a bottom-up approach" |
| Strategic Proposals | Engagement scoping, deliverables, pricing, SOWs | "Draft a proposal for a digital transformation engagement" |
| Workshop Design | Facilitation frameworks, agenda design, stakeholder alignment | "Design a two-day strategy offsite for a leadership team" |
| Transformation Roadmaps | Phased implementation plans, KPIs, governance structures | "Create a 12-month digital transformation roadmap" |
| Frameworks | Porter's Five Forces, McKinsey 7S, BCG matrix, and more | "Apply Porter's Five Forces to the online grocery market" |
| Benchmarking | Peer comparison, operational KPIs, best practices | "Benchmark this company's operations against industry leaders" |

## Quick Start

1. "Size the market for AI-powered legal tools in North America."
2. "Draft a consulting proposal for a supply chain optimization project."
3. "Build a transformation roadmap for a bank's digital migration."
