# /investment

Investment analysis, due diligence, and portfolio management for finance professionals.

## Available Capabilities

| Area | What it does | Example prompt |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Due Diligence | Full commercial, financial, and operational DD frameworks | "Run a commercial due diligence on this SaaS company" |
| DCF Valuation | Discounted cash flow models with scenario analysis | "Build a DCF for a company with these financials" |
| Risk Frameworks | Investment risk scoring, red flag detection, sensitivity tables | "Assess the key risks for this acquisition target" |
| Portfolio Analysis | Portfolio construction, allocation, and performance attribution | "Analyze the diversification of this portfolio" |
| Comparable Analysis | Trading comps, precedent transactions, multiples benchmarking | "Find comparable transactions for a B2B SaaS exit" |
| Investment Thesis | Structured thesis development with bull/bear scenarios | "Draft an investment thesis for entering this market" |

## Quick Start

1. "I have a company's financials. Help me run a full due diligence."
2. "Build a three-scenario DCF model for a company growing at 25% with 40% margins."
3. "Create a risk matrix for a Series B investment in a healthcare startup."
