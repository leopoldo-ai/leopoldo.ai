# /intelligence

Competitor analysis, market positioning, and C-suite intelligence reports.

## Available Capabilities

| Area | What it does | Example prompt |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Competitor Analysis | Deep dives on competitors, product mapping, strategy teardowns | "Analyze our top 3 competitors and their go-to-market strategies" |
| Market Positioning | Positioning maps, differentiation analysis, white space identification | "Map the competitive landscape for enterprise AI platforms" |
| C-Suite Reports | Executive briefings, board-ready intelligence summaries | "Create a quarterly competitive intelligence brief for the board" |
| Win/Loss Analysis | Deal outcome patterns, competitive displacement tracking | "Analyze why we're losing deals to competitor X" |
| Trend Monitoring | Industry signals, emerging threats, technology shifts | "What are the key trends reshaping the fintech landscape?" |
| Strategic Alerts | Early warning systems, trigger events, M&A tracking | "Set up a monitoring framework for competitor moves in our space" |

## Quick Start

1. "Build a competitive landscape map for the project management software market."
2. "Draft an executive briefing on our top competitor's recent strategy shifts."
3. "Analyze the positioning gap between us and the market leader."
