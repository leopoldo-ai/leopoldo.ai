# /markets

Macro analysis, backtesting, and portfolio optimization for traders and portfolio managers.

## Available Capabilities

| Area | What it does | Example prompt |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Macro Analysis | Economic regime detection, cross-asset signals, central bank tracking | "Analyze the current macro environment and its implications for equities" |
| Backtesting | Strategy validation, historical simulation, performance metrics | "Backtest a momentum strategy on S&P 500 sectors over 10 years" |
| Portfolio Optimization | Mean-variance, risk parity, factor-based allocation | "Optimize this portfolio for maximum Sharpe ratio with constraints" |
| Trading Frameworks | Entry/exit signals, position sizing, risk management rules | "Design a systematic trend-following framework for futures" |
| Market Screening | Cross-asset screening, relative value, anomaly detection | "Screen for undervalued European equities using quality factors" |
| Risk Analytics | VaR, stress testing, drawdown analysis, correlation regimes | "Run a stress test on this portfolio using 2008 and 2020 scenarios" |

## Quick Start

1. "What is the macro regime right now, and how should I position a multi-asset portfolio?"
2. "Backtest a 60/40 portfolio with quarterly rebalancing over the last 20 years."
3. "Design a risk management framework for a concentrated equity book."
