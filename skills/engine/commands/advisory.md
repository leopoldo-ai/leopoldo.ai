# /advisory

Pitch books, sell-side advisory, and capital markets execution for investment bankers.

## Available Capabilities

| Area | What it does | Example prompt |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Pitch Books | Management presentations, teasers, and CIMs | "Create a pitch book structure for a sell-side mandate" |
| DCF and Valuation | Full valuation suites with football field charts | "Build a valuation summary with DCF, comps, and precedents" |
| Sell-Side Advisory | Process letters, buyer lists, data room organization | "Draft a process letter for a controlled auction" |
| Capital Markets | Debt and equity offering analysis, pricing, and structuring | "Analyze the optimal capital structure for this issuer" |
| Fairness Opinions | Fairness analysis frameworks, board presentations | "Outline the key components for a fairness opinion" |
| Client Coverage | Industry mapping, relationship tracking, opportunity pipeline | "Map the key M&A themes in European healthcare" |

## Quick Start

1. "Help me structure a CIM for a mid-market industrial company."
2. "Build a buyer universe for a 200M revenue software business."
3. "Create a valuation football field with three methodologies."
