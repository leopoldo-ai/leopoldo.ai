# Essentials — Universal Skill Pack

Il pack fondamentale per ogni professionista. 28 skill trasversali che coprono strategia, governance, decisioni, orchestrazione, reporting, comunicazione, document intelligence, ricerca, miglioramento continuo e knowledge management.

## Target

- Qualsiasi professionista (finance, consulting, tech, healthcare)
- Pack base — install this + your domain-specific pack(s)
- Prerequisito per tutti i pack verticali

## Skill incluse

### Business & Strategy (5 skills)

| Skill | Scopo | Priorita' |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| `strategy-advisor` | Framework strategici: SWOT, Porter, Ansoff, Blue Ocean, scenario planning, competitive positioning | P0 |
| `ceo-advisor` | Governance, board preparation, organizational design, stakeholder management, capital allocation, crisis leadership | P0 |
| `product-manager-toolkit` | RICE scoring, backlog, PRD, prioritization | P1 |
| `feature-impact-analyzer` | Decision matrix RICE + multi-perspective (Product, Tech, Finance, Strategy, UX) | P1 |
| `decision-toolkit` | Weighted decision matrix, ROI, cost-benefit, make-vs-buy, sensitivity analysis | P1 |
