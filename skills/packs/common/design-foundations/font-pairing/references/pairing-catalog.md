# Font Pairing Catalog — 12 Curated Combinations

Each pairing includes heading + body fonts, recommended weights, scale ratio, and use-case guidance.

---

## 1. Playfair Display + Source Sans 3

**Classification:** Transitional Serif + Humanist Sans
**Style:** Editorial, luxury, sophisticated
**Best for:** Luxury brands, editorial publications, high-end finance, law firms

| Role | Font | Weights | Size |
|------|------|---------|------|
| Heading | Playfair Display | 600, 700 | Scale ratio 1.333 |
| Body | Source Sans 3 | 400, 600 | Base 16px |

**Why it works:** High stroke contrast in Playfair creates drama; Source Sans 3's humanist forms provide comfortable reading. The contrast between decorative serif and clean sans creates clear hierarchy.
